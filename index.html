About NexGen Corenova

NexGen Corenova Tech Solutions is a specialist operations and ERP consulting firm. We do not sell software, build platforms, or develop applications. Key Differentiator

Country-Specific Customs Process Analysis & BRD / FRD Development

One of our strongest capabilities — and what sets us apart — is our ability to analyse customs and trade compliance processes across different countries and convert them into structured software requirement documents.

🌍

What We Do

  • 01 Study the customs clearance process, trade regulations, and compliance requirements specific to a target country
  • 02 Map the process flows, document types, declaration formats, and system integration points required
  • 03 Convert the analysis into a structured BRD and FRD that a software development team can use directly to build or extend their customs module
  • 04 Co-ordinate with the development team through the build cycle to ensure the solution aligns with the documented requirements
Important Note

Scope and depth of analysis for specific countries may be subject to local support requirements. Where local documentation or in-country collaboration is needed, this will be assessed and communicated transparently at the outset.

🏢

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our services and approach.

Do you sell or build software?
+
No. We are a pure consulting firm — we do not sell software, build applications, or recommend specific platforms for commercial gain. We analyse your operations, identify gaps, and deliver documentation. If you need co-ordination with a software team (yours or a vendor's), we handle that too — but we are never the developer.
What exactly do you deliver at the end of an engagement?
+
Our primary deliverable is always a document — a Gap Analysis Report, Business Requirement Document (BRD), Functional Requirement Document (FRD), or Process Flow Map. These give your technology team — whether in-house or outsourced — everything they need to build or fix the right solution.
How do you work with logistics software providers?
+
Software providers engage us to represent them with their clients. We step in as the domain expert — analysing the client's operational needs, preparing the requirement documents, and co-ordinating between the client and the development team. This lets the software provider focus on building while we handle the business analysis layer.
Can you handle customs requirements for any country?
+
We have strong capability in analysing country-specific customs processes and converting them into BRD/FRD documents. The scope for specific countries may be subject to local support requirements — where local documentation or in-country collaboration is needed, this is assessed and communicated clearly at the start of the engagement.
How quickly can you start on a project?
+
After an initial consultation call to understand your requirements, we can typically begin the discovery and planning phase within 1–2 weeks depending on project scope and resource availability.
