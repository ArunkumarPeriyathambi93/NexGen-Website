About NexGen Corenova
Built on Expertise. Driven by Results.

NexGen Corenova Tech Solutions was founded to solve a problem that most technology companies ignore — the gap between how logistics businesses operate and how their ERP systems are built, implemented and maintained.

Our core focus is ERP consulting, working on both sides of the equation — as the trusted advisor for end-user companies navigating ERP selection and implementation, and as a domain expert supporting ERP software vendors who need real logistics expertise to build better products.

Beyond ERP, our team brings together 12+ years of expertise in IT services, security systems and electronics — giving clients a genuinely complete technology partner rather than a single-service vendor.

We serve clients across India and globally, with a proven track record spanning India customs, transport, warehousing, international courier, e-commerce and retail — and international exposure including Australia customs ERP systems.
